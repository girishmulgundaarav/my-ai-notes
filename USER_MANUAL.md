## Getting Started

### Accessing the Web App
1. Navigate to the login screen where you can authenticate using your registered **Email & Password** or connect using your **Google account**.
2. If you don't have an account, click the **Sign Up** tab to create one.
3. Once logged in, you will be taken to your dashboard.

---

## Dashboard (Home)

The Dashboard acts as your productivity command center, offering high-level statistics, quick-capture features, and interactive widgets.

- **Workspace Statistics**: At the top, view your productivity metrics, including *Total Notes*, *Total Words*, and *AI Enhanced Count*.
- **Quick Capture**: Instantly jot down drafts. Type your thoughts in the text field and click **Capture** to save it directly to your general notes folder without leaving the home screen.
- **Recently Edited Carousel**: View and quickly navigate to your top 3 recently updated notes. Each card shows the cover image, title, snippet, category tag, and last edit timestamp.

---

## Managing Your Notes (Notes Shelf)

Access the **My Notes** page from the sidebar navigation to view and search your full note collection.

- **Dynamic Search**: Use the search bar at the top to filter notes by titles or content keywords in real-time.
- **Category Filtering**: Organize notes using the filter tabs at the top:
  - `All` — View all notes.
  - `General` — Miscellaneous notes and quick captures.
  - `Work` — Professional tasks, meetings, and work notes.
  - `Personal` — Personal journals, thoughts, and plans.
  - `Ideas` — Creative brainstorming, project designs, and innovations.
- **Quick Actions**: Hover over any note card in the grid to **Edit** (pencil icon) or **Delete** (trash icon) that specific note.

## Creating & Editing Notes (Rich Editor)

Access the editor by clicking the **Create Note** menu in the sidebar or clicking **Create New Note** on the dashboard.

- **Title & Category**: Give your note a title and assign a category tag from the dropdown menu (`General`, `Work`, `Personal`, or `Ideas`).
- **Cover Image & Attachments**: 
  - Click **Add Cover** / **Change Cover** to upload a banner image for your note card.
  - Click **Attach Files** to upload relevant documents, images, or PDFs to the note.
- **Rich Formatting Toolbar**: Use toolbar shortcuts to format text:
  - **Bold** (`**text**`)
  - *Italics* (`_text_`)
  - <u>Underline</u> (`<u>text</u>`)
  - Bulleted List (`- item`)
  - Link (`[title](url)`)
- **Live Preview Toggler**: Switch between the text **Editor** and the **Preview** mode (which renders full markdown, HTML raw embeds, and structured layouts).

## AI Integration & Features

AI Notes leverages advanced AI technologies (OpenAI GPT models) to make note management and knowledge retrieval fast and intuitive.

### 1. Quick AI Assistant (Dashboard)
Ask questions across your entire note collection.
- Enter any query in the assistant bar (e.g., *"What were the ideas I had for the dashboard design last week?"*).
- The assistant retrieves all relevant note context, processes the query, and formats a synthesized response.

### 2. AI Editor Enhance (Editor)
Refine, expand, and structure your note content.
- Click the **AI Enhance** button in the editor toolbar.
- The assistant fixes grammar, resolves spelling errors, improves readability, and auto-formats raw text into beautiful, structured markdown (using headers, bold text, and bullet lists).
- Compare the side-by-side **AI Suggestion Panel** and choose to **Replace Original** or **Discard**.

## Integrated Productivity Tools

AI Notes features key productivity widgets located on the Dashboard to help manage your time and progress.

### 1. Pomodoro Timer
A time management tool designed to increase focus.
- **Modes**: Choose between **Focus Session** (25 minutes), **Short Break** (5 minutes), and **Long Break** (15 minutes).
- **Control**: Start, pause, or reset the timer. Upon completion, an audio beep alert plays.
- **Log Tracking**: Completed focus sessions are logged, updating your **Weekly Focus Minutes** counter at the bottom of the widget.

### 2. Dashboard Task Checklist
A daily checklist to keep track of tasks.
- **Add Tasks**: Type a task in the field and press Enter.
- **AI Task Breakdown**: Click the **Sparkle icon** next to a task. The AI will automatically break down your high-level task into **3 actionable, concrete sub-tasks** inside a nested checklist.
- **Delete Tasks**: Click the **Trash icon** next to a task to delete it.

### 3. Reading Shelf & Tracker
Keep track of your current books and literature.
- **Add a Book**: Enter the *Title*, *Author*, and *Total Pages*. You can also link the book to an existing note (e.g., your reading summaries) from the dropdown list.
- **Update Page Progress**: Click on your current page count to update it. The progress bar will automatically adjust showing your reading percent.
- **Delete a Book**: Remove a book from your shelf by clicking the trash icon next to it.

## Account Management

Manage your personal profile and preferences on the **Account** page.

- **Profile Picture**: Click the **Camera icon** to upload or update your avatar. Old avatars are automatically deleted to keep storage clean.
- **User Initials / Avatar Badging**: Your profile picture or initials will be shown on notes you update.
- **Account Details**: Check your email address and authentication provider (e.g., Google login vs. Email/Password sign in).
- **Signing Out**: Click **Sign Out of AI Notes** to end your session.
